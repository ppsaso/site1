What is the best city for a first casino resort trip in the USA?

Las Vegas is often the easiest starting point because it offers the widest mix of hotel styles, dining, entertainment, and direct flight access.

Are these listings focused on hotels rather than online gambling?

Yes. Casino Hotels USA is positioned as a travel and hospitality guide centered on resort stays, amenities, destinations, and entertainment experiences.

How should I compare casino resorts?
