Top Atlantic City Casino Resorts for a Weekend Getaway

Editorial Team May 14, 2026 6 min read

Boardwalk hotels, marina stays, and how to pick the right Atlantic City resort for a short break.

Atlantic City works especially well for short leisure trips because the destination is compact and easy to navigate. Travelers can prioritize ocean views, direct boardwalk access, nightlife, or larger integrated resorts depending on their schedule.

Boardwalk properties offer the strongest sense of place, especially for visitors who want to mix resort time with walking, dining, and live entertainment nearby. Marina-area resorts can feel more self-contained and are often a good fit for guests who prefer staying mostly on property.

A two-night itinerary benefits from a hotel that reduces transit time. Choosing a resort with a broad food and beverage mix, an indoor spa or pool option, and evening entertainment can make the whole trip smoother.
